Welcome to Socialist Appeal! We are the official publication of the International Marxist Tendency in the United States. In addition to Marxist theory, we provide news and analysis on politics, economics, and the labor movement in the US and internationally. We fight for a labor party in the United States and international socialism. Join us to build the forces of Marxism in the US!
